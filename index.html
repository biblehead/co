What Is BibleHead

The word is
the way.

Biblical illiteracy is real. A lot of people know about God but have never sat down and read what He actually said. BibleHead exists to change that.

This is a safe space to learn and understand the Bible, together. Whether you grew up in church or you are picking up a Bible for the first time, you are welcome here. We read it. We study it. We talk about it. We let it speak.

To eradicate biblical illiteracy by encouraging people to read the Bible for themselves, and get to know God through the word.

The podcast, the community, the resources, the merch — all of it points back to one thing. Get in the word. The word is the way.
